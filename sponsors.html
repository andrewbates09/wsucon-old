Sponsors

While the Linux Users' Group puts in a lot of work to make WSUCon happen, these are the people that have really made it a gaming con. Without them prizes, food, and other goodies just wouldn't be there!

Thank you for making WSUCon possible! Every year we are able to put on this completely free event is another great year for participants! We strive for WSUCon to grow and forever be free of charge.


Our event comes at a considerable cost and as such, we are asking for your help through sponsoring the event. Official sponsors will be mentioned on all advertisements and publications, as well as announced during the event.

If you would like to sponsor this amazing event, please contact us so that we can make this event as amazing as possible!
