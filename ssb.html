The Tournament Rules

General Rules

1. You must be registered for the tournament.
2. Double Elimination tournament
3. Matches are best of 3, with losers finals, winners finals, and grand finals being best of 5.
4. Items off.
5. 4 Stock, 8 minutes time.
a. In the scenario where neither player dies after 8 minutes, the player with the lowest percent, or most stock wins.

Striking Procedure

1. Players determine who strikes first (rock paper scissors)
2. Players strike stages in alternating order until one stage remains, which the players will play on. This is for the first match only.

Set Procedure

1. Players may request a double blind pick to eliminate character counter picking in the first match.
2. The loser of the first match (and of successive matches) chooses the next stage, and then the winner chooses his character, and then the loser chooses his character.
3. The winner is permitted to ban 1 stage. There are no bans in a best of 5 match.
4. A player may not counterpick a stage which he or she has previously won on.
5. Wobbling is legal, wobbling past 250% is considered stalling and is not permitted.
6. Players may agree on other rules between each other for a match, but these will not be enforced.
