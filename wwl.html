Welcome to WinterWonderLAN!

Where: CUE 202
When: November 6th-7th, 2015, 5pm-5pm
Cost: FREE!!! (but bring money for food)

What is WinterWonderLAN?

We treat Winter WonderLAN as our pilot for WSUCon, with a focus of testing new ideas for WSUCon, while keeping our costs low. This way we can go all out for WSUCon.

What should we expect?

As always, we will have movies playing, games happening, and a large room with gigabit ethernet. We will NOT be providing pizza, although we will try to get collections going to get some through the night.

What do I need to bring?

- Yourself
- A computer, power, ethernet cable
- Money if you wish to get food.
- Energy
